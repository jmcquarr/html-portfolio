Jonathan's Movie Musings

Sharing my thoughts on some recent movies I've seen.

The Menu

★★★★★

This movie was so tense! I loved it. It might start off slow, but every new course cranks up the heat, culminating in a fiery finale that has to be seen to be believed!

Bullet Train

★★★☆☆

I had low hopes for this one, but it ended up being a really enjoyable ride! The actors were all charming, and I loved how all the different stories came together by the end.

Avatar 2: The Way of Water

★★★★☆

What can I say about this one that hasn't already been said? The plot may not have been anything revolutionary, but the film had a solid emotional core that drew me in, and the visuals were stunning! I saw it in 3D, and it really felt like I was being transported to another world.
